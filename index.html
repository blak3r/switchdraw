What's Switchdraw?

SwitchDraw is a fun drawing game that will challenge you to visualize something from a crazy doodle or squiggle. You can play by yourself or with as many friends as you like.

Speed of Thought

You can't unimagine. Once you imagined it. it's there. Where ever ideas go faster than the speed of thought. Fast is a funny word. Youc an go fast and you can hold fast. it means almost the opposite thing in either case.

What about thinking fast? Sometimes the speed of though is pretty slow... Some drarned thing blocks the process.

Conquer Your Mental Blocks

Switchdraw is here to rescue your mind. You'll have so much fun I promise you won't notice when, but you'll be jumping over those stubborn mental blocks.

If you need a good imagination? (Who doesn't?) It could be the difference between gettin' it and loosing' it.
